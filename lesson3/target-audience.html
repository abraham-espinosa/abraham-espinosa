Target Audience

Who: Those people who live in the areas of of Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven, Idaho. They want to know the weather for the next few days to plan their activites with confidense and avoid uncomfortable situacions because the weather.

Age: 18 to 50

Income: Income levels will typically be between $0 to $50K

Motivation: Have a good view of what kind of weather may be in the next few days to be prepared (clothes, places, etc) and good places to view according to the weather.

Education: High School or higher.

Family Status: Married or single.

Where, when and how the visitor will be accessing the site: Any time through smartphone and computer.
