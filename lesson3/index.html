Site Purpose

The main purpose of "Tell me Weather" is to provide current weather and forecasts for the areas of Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven, Idaho. Our forecasts will be accurate and people will be able to plan their week with confidence and prevent uncomfortable situations. Also, we will give ideas of the best places (restaurants, parks, museums, stores, etc.) people can visit according to the weather.
